WheelAccess

WheelAccess is an intelligent crowd-sourcing platform which uses sensors to automatically record your wheelchair trips and allows anyone to discover wheelchair accessible routes in Singapore.

Through this platform, we hope to make Singapore a more inclusive place for wheelchair users, improving their mobility.

About

WheelAccess was developed by Code for Philly, an open group of citizens working to harness the power of technology to modernize citizenship in Philadelphia.

WheelAccess users are helping planners in the City of Philadelphia and Delaware Valley build better bike routes.
